How It Works
From nearby to connected
01

Find People Around You

Open Proxima and instantly see who's nearby — in your class, café, gym, or event. Real people, right around you, right now.

02

Like or Get Liked

Browse nearby profiles and like the ones that catch your eye. Others can like you too — all quietly, with no pressure.

03

Appear on Their Radar

Once you like someone, your blurred profile appears on their radar. They'll know someone nearby is interested — without knowing who, until they like back.

04

Match & Meet

When they like you back, it's a match. They're already in the room — say hi in real life and let it happen naturally.

Why Proxima
Built different, by design
📍

People Around You Only

You only see people physically near you right now — in your building, your café, your event. Not strangers from across the city.

🫧

Blurred Until Matched

Your profile stays blurred on someone's radar until they like you back. Mutual interest only — no one-sided exposure.

🔒

Encrypted Connection

All communication between matched users is end-to-end encrypted. Your conversations stay between you and your match — nobody else.

💬

Minimal Chat by Design

Message limits push you toward real-life interaction instead of getting stuck talking online forever.

🎯

Mutual Interest Only

Nobody knows you liked them until they like you back. No awkward one-sided notifications, no pressure on either side.

⚡

No Pay-to-Win

No boosted profiles or subscription tiers that hide your likes. Just the people around you, shown fairly — nothing hidden.
